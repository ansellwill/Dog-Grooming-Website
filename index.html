We've got what you need!

Our home dog grooming salon is situated in Spencerswood Reading, with top of the range facilities including a brand new Hydrobath. Groomingdales is owned and managed by a fully qualified groomer. A complete range of services are available by appointment, from bathing and drying to scissoring and hand stripping. We also have walk in appointments available for small treatments such as nail clipping. At Groomingdales we recognise that every dog is different. Therefore at every appointment you will have a chance to meet with the stylist to discuss your dog's grooming requirements so that we can provide the best possible treatment for your dog's specific needs. Your dog will be pampered in a friendly, safe and relaxed home environment, and due to individual grooming appoinments your dog will have one to one attention throughout, without unnecessary waiting around.
